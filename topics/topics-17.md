# Peer Teaching Practicum

## Phase 3 Outline

- Deliver the module in a classroom-like setting
- Receive structured critique from peers and facilitators
- Refine the module one last time
- Leave with classroom-ready materials and a final implementation plan

## What This Phase Is For

This is the in-person final week. The emphasis is on teaching, feedback, and final polish.
